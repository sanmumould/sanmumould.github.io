About Us

Huangshi Sanmu Plastic Mould Co., Ltd. is located in Huangshi City, an open city along the Yangtze River. It was established in 1991. It is a professional manufacturer of plastic profile extrusion moulds integrating design, research and development, manufacturing, commissioning, sales and technical services. . The company has more than 200 sets of production and processing equipment, 2 mold debugging production lines, annual production capacity of more than 1,200 sets, large-scale plant area. The company has concentrated a group of high-level professional designers and experienced mold debugging personnel, including 4 senior engineers, more than 30 engineers and senior technicians. The company has a complete CAD graphics system, which uses CAD/CAM networking to run and implements computerized system management. For more than ten years, our company has successfully developed all kinds of high-speed molds with high level. The main products are: PVC door and window profile mould, PVC decorative material micro-foam profile mould, PVC co-extrusion, aluminum-plastic composite mould. It is a good enterprise with excellent equipment and complete categories in the same industry. The company always regards quality as the life of the company. In November 2003, the company successfully passed the ISO9001:2000 quality system certification, and strictly adopted ISO9001 quality management and enterprise management to ensure product quality and meet the requirements of users.

1. Product Introduction This mold is suitable for the development and production of plastic chemical building materials such as profiles, pipes, plates and decorative profiles. Various specifications of high speed extrusion dies have been developed. For more than ten years, the mold industry has continued to grow and develop, and now has a high level of technology and special electrical processing equipment for molds.

2. Product specifications and performance The plastic profile extrusion die developed by the company from the casement window series 50, 58, 60, 75, push-pull series 60, 62, 70, 75, 80, 88, 95, etc. and the American window series 64, 85, 88, 93 and other series specifications and types. In addition, successful co-extrusion molds such as soft and hard coextrusion, color coextrusion, core foam coextrusion, and double coextrusion have been developed, as well as other extrusion dies. Extrusion molds play an important role in profile extrusion systems. The quality of the mold directly affects the appearance, shape, wall thickness, size, flatness, yield, and physical and mechanical properties of the profile. The mold structure is generally divided into a die (a parison of a profiled profile), a sizing die and a water tank (mainly cooling and shaping the parison extruded by the die); the mold design and processing requires accurate analysis and calculation of plastic operating conditions and rheology. The relationship with temperature.

3. Product application field This product is mainly used in the field of chemical building materials, for the production of various specifications, color profiles and decorative profiles. The molds developed and produced by the company have accounted for more than 30% of the market share. The products have been successfully applied to nearly 400 profile production enterprises in 30 provinces, municipalities and autonomous regions, and actively expand the international market, and have successively exported to many countries and regions. Wide range of praise from internal and external users.
